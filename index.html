About Us

Cognarena Technologies is a dynamic startup dedicated to fostering innovation in technology and education. Our Academic Division offers engaging courses on Robotics, IoT, and AI, enhanced with gamification to ensure interactive and effective learning.

Our R&D Department specializes in designing and developing Special Purpose Machines (SPMs) and customized projects for schools and colleges. Additionally, our Electronics Store provides high-quality components and cutting-edge tech solutions.

As a budding startup, we are committed to bridging the gap between learning and practical application, empowering students and professionals to excel in the ever-evolving tech landscape.

Our Services

We offer technical training, R&D solutions, and electronics sales under one roof. Our services include gamified courses on Robotics, IoT, and AI, development of SPMs and school/college projects—including 3D printing services—and a store for quality electronic components.
